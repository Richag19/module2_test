My Garden Palnts

Plant1-Singonium

Due to the shape of it's leaf it is called also Arrow Head Plant. A beautiful, easy to grow plant. It can be placed as indoor or as well as outdoor plant. It likes moisture, can be propagated with stem part in water or in soil like money plants. If it get support of moss stick, it's areal roots stick on it and plant thrive best. It should be kept in bright light but not in direct sun light. Needs less care and less fertilizer.

Plant2-Rose

Roses are the most attractive flowers. They have vibrant colours and lovely fragrance that nobody can avoid. Every garden lover have rose plants in their collection. They give flowers almost round the year. It is a perennial plant. Rose plant needs bright sun-light, timely fertilizer and pruning. Otherwise after some time there is a common complaint about non flowering plant or not growing very well. They need good fertilizer rich in nitrogen, phosphorus and potassium every month.

Plant3-Portuleca

The most easy to grow, propagate and filling small herbaceous plants. Their colours and look attract everyone. Like grass, they don't need much care. In summers as well as in winters, there varieties bloom in garden of plant lovers. Parsulane variety grow in summers, and portuleca and moss-rose bloom in winters also.
